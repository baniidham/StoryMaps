# Type Relevant Incentives/Policy Benefit Type Impact on Location Decision Making
1. Fiscal Digital Ecosystem Acceleration ("DESAC") – Digital Infrastructure Provider
  • Investment Tax Credit of 100% on capital expenditure that could offset up to 100% of statutory income for up to 10 years
2. Economic Recovery Plan ("PENJANA")
  • Income tax rate of 0% to 10% for a period of up to 10 years
3. Accelerated Capital Allowance ("ACA") – ICT Service
  • Initial allowance of 40% and an annual allowance of 20% for expenditure incurred in acquiring computers and ICT assets
4. Fiscal Malaysia Digital Status – Tech Enabler
  • Grant of 30-50% of total project cost or up to RM1 Million whichever is lower
* Incentives are assessed and granted on a case-by-case basis
5. Green Investment Tax Allowance ("ITA")
  • ITA of 100% on qualifying capital expenditure incurred by green technology projects for 3 years from the data of the first qualifying CAPEX incurred. This can be offset against 70% of statutory income in the year of assessment.
6. Green Investment Tax Exemption ("ITE")
  • ITE of 70% on statutory income for qualifying green services
7. Solar Leasing Investment Tax Exemption ("ITE")
  • ITE of 70% on statutory income for solar leasing activities for a period of up to 10 years of assessment
8. Non-Fiscal Malaysia Digital Status – Tech Enabler
  • Foreign equity restriction exemptions
  • Allowance to employ foreign knowledge workers
