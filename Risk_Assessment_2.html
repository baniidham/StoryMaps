# Parameter Commentary Risk Level
1. Financial Risk
  • The ongoing challenges faced by the Forest City project, including its slow sales and environmental concerns, would likely impact the firm's financial standing.
  • Nonetheless, it is worth noting that the backing of the Johor government and the Sultan may alleviate much of its financial concerns.
2. Credit Risk
  • There is no track record of the credit worthiness of Esplanade Danga 88.
  • However, the Johor government has a strong credit rating. According to RAM Ratings, a Malaysian ratings agency, medium term notes recently issued by Johor Corporation was assigned an AAA/Stable Rating.
3. Project Risk
  • At the end of August 2023, Prime Minister Anwar Ibrahim revealed plans to make Forest City a special financial zone, with incentives including a 15% income tax rate for skilled workers and multiple entry visas.
  • However, Esplanade Danga 88's strong dependency on Country Garden for the development of the project creates potential challenges should the Chinese developer default.
4. Management Risk
  • There has not been widespread reports of specific management concerns of Esplanade Danga 88.
